一、为什么是现在Why now

Satoshi's lens
一个有真正主权的存在，不能建立在可信第三方之上。你的品牌、你的观众、你的叙事，不该由别人的推荐引擎决定生死。短视频教会了世界"人人可表达"，却也证明了"平台即房东"的脆弱——一夜限流，资产归零。独立站是密码朋克精神在品牌维度的落地：点对点地拥有，而非恳求算法施舍。
A truly sovereign presence cannot be built on a trusted third party. Your brand, your audience, your narrative should not live or die by someone else's recommendation engine. Short video taught the world that anyone can express — yet it also proved the fragility of "the platform is the landlord": one algorithm change and the asset goes to zero. The independent site is cypherpunk philosophy applied to the dimension of brand: own it peer-to-peer, rather than begging the algorithm for alms.
Jobs' lens
我们生来是要在这个宇宙里留下痕迹的。一个品牌不该只是一张被刷过去的卡片，它该是一座你亲手建的数字据点。别人给你流量，我们给你据点。Insanely great 的独立站，不该只有大公司建得起——现在，任何人一次对话就能造出。
We're here to put a dent in the universe. A brand should not be a card someone swipes past; it should be a digital stronghold you build with your own hands. Others give you traffic; we give you the stronghold. An insanely great independent site should not be the privilege of big companies alone — now, anyone can build one in a single conversation.

二、我们在建造什么What we are building

nexl 是一套"双核"建站操作系统——先凝品牌灵魂（broker），再长成站点（builder），且每次迭代守护同一声音。模板库（templates/）是其中可复用的集体资产：每个模板带 machine-readable 的三维评级（quality / cost_efficiency / stability），被激活的 agent 会在建站时自动调出该平台最优骨架。你不是在从空白页起步，而是在巨人肩上起步。
nexl is a dual-core site-building OS — it first distills the brand soul (broker), then grows the site (builder), and guards the same voice on every iteration. The template library (templates/) is its reusable collective asset: every template carries a machine-readable 3-axis rating (quality / cost_efficiency / stability), and the activated agent automatically recalls the best scaffold for the platform when building. You don't start from a blank page; you start on the shoulders of giants.
